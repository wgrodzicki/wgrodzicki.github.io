Hello, I'm Wojciech Grodzicki. I'm a game programmer.

It hasn't always been the case, though. My original background is in life sciences, specifically in food technology and toxicology. I was working as a university researcher when I re-descovered my childhood passion for video games. But besides playing I started tinkering and modding them myself. Soon I felt that in order to achieve what I wanted, I had to learn how to code. So I started my first programming course. I was hooked - and I still am.

After one year on learning on my own I was fortunate enough to join an indie game studio as an intern. At that time I also realized that I needed to fill many gaps in my computer science knowledge, so I decided to go back to school and enrolled in a post-graduate programme in Software Engineering.

Now I work on real-life projects as a Junior Game Programmer, focusing on C# and Unity. Every day I'm thrilled at the prospect of building interactive experiences and learning more about the fascintating intersection of technology and art known as game development!
